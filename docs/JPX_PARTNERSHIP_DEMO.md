# JPX Partnership Concept Demo Narrative

## Mandatory framing

Do not use JPX logos, imitate JPX pages, call the product official/approved, or imply that displayed market/company data came from JPX. All companies, codes, values, market segments, services, and factors in this demo are synthetic. A fixed on-screen disclaimer is not required.

## Ten-step explanation order

### 1. 日本市場の現状課題

Start with the operational problem: sustainability data, evidence, units, organizational boundaries, and review status are spread across departments and are recollected for different reporting needs. Show the landing problem statement and explain that the concept focuses on the work between information publication and a reliable disclosure package.

### 2. TERRASTによる初期入力

Open 日本未来製造株式会社 and the Data Sync Center. State clearly that `MockTerrastConnector` uses synthetic records shaped for the demonstration. Show three years of existing data, source IDs, periods, units, confidence, and dry-run preview. Do not describe mock fields as the real TERRAST API.

### 3. 不足情報だけを企業が補完

Show added/updated/conflict/unchanged classifications. Resolve one manual-versus-mock conflict with an explicit choice and reason. Then show the remaining gaps so the company works on missing or weak-evidence data rather than starting from zero.

### 4. SSBJ／ISSBワークフロー

Open a versioned requirement with an original short summary, source URL, mapped metrics, response, evidence, owner, status, and history. Answer a plain-language guided question, create an AI/demo-AI draft labeled 「AI提案・要レビュー」, submit, return with reason, revise, and approve. Explain that readiness is not a legal compliance judgment.

### 5. Scope 3とサプライチェーン

Show Scope 1/2/3 trends and 15-category coverage, distinguishing measured/estimated values and demo factors. Create/copy the presentation-only demo supplier link, submit synthetic evidence metadata, return or accept it, and show response/quality impact. Explain that real expiring/token-hashed invitations need the production server command; no real email or supplier data is used.

### 6. 移行計画

Follow current state → risk/opportunity → target → action → CapEx/OpEx → KPI → progress. Show the fictional transition risk and equipment-electrification action, including governance oversight and evidence gaps. Avoid asserting a forecast or financed outcome.

### 7. JPX側の市場全体分析

Switch to Platform Operator Demo Admin. Show fictional aggregate participation, readiness distributions, common gaps, overdue/review workload, Scope coverage, transition-plan rate, and supplier responses. Demonstrate that non-public company details are hidden and that only a scoped, active consent exposes a company summary.

### 8. 投資家・金融機関へのデータ活用

Explain the potential value of consistent units, periods, provenance, readiness, transition actions, and evidence status for downstream analysis. This is a future data-product concept; no investor feed, financing offer, suitability judgment, or real financial product is claimed. Marketplace cards are fictional.

### 9. 全国共通基盤としての拡張性

Use the architecture view: versioned catalogs, normalized metrics, repository/connector ports, consent separation, multi-tenant RLS, private evidence, and auditable workflow. Explain how additional frameworks or authorized connectors could be added without changing the core workflow. Do not claim nationwide adoption.

### 10. 正式提携に向けた次段階

Close with decisions required for a next phase:

- validate the operational problem and prioritized listed-company cohort;
- agree roles, governance, consent purposes, aggregate privacy thresholds, and operating owner;
- execute an authorized TERRAST interface/data-rights discovery;
- agree an SSBJ/ISSB catalog curation and update process;
- run a limited pilot with synthetic or properly contracted data;
- complete security/privacy/legal/accessibility and operating-model review;
- define measurable outcomes, support/SLA, funding, procurement, and exit criteria;
- approve any public claims, naming, branding, and partnership announcement separately.

The ask is permission to explore and validate the next stage—not recognition that a partnership already exists.

## Suggested evidence to capture

For each step, record the screen/route, test or interaction result, synthetic-data label, relevant file, and verified Preview URL. Capture the consent-denied and cross-tenant-denied cases as strongly as the happy path; trust is part of the product value.
